## Reporting Problems in the Woods

The park and woodlands are managed by <a href="http://www.midsussex.gov.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Mid Sussex District Council</a>.

Please report any problems or concerns to the Ranger team at [parksopenspaces@midsussex.gov.uk](mailto:parksopenspaces@midsussex.gov.uk?cc=info@egmel.co.uk), or contact them on [01444 477392](tel:+44-1444-477392). Ideally include a [what3words](https://what3words.com) location and copy in the EGMEL contact email: [info@egmel.co.uk](mailto:info@egmel.co.uk).
